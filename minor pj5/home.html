MOTIHARI COLLEGE OF Engineering(government college)


Motihari College of Engineering was established in 1980, it is a government college which is fully funded by the Government of Bihar. The college is affiliated with the Aryabhatta Knowledge University (AKU) and is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). The college currently offers B.Tech courses in 4 different disciplines which are Computer Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. The institute also plans to start the M.Tech program in Computer Science & Engineering from July 2020 onwards after receiving mandatory approval from the AICTE.
